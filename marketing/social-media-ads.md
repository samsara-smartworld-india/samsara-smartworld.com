## Campaign Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor

| Metric | Target (Le Meridien) | Target (Plot 87) |
|---|---|---|
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 impressions) | < INR 150 | < INR 120 |
| CTR (Click-through rate) | > 1.5% | > 1.8% |
| CPC (Cost per click) | < INR 15 | < INR 12 |
| CPL (Cost per lead) | < INR 500 | < INR 400 |
| Leads/month | 15-30 | 25-50 |
| Site visit conversion | 20% of leads | 20% of leads |

### Optimisation Schedule

- **Week 1:** Launch all ads, monitor delivery and spend
- **Week 2:** Pause underperforming ads (CTR < 0.8%), increase budget on top performers
- **Week 3:** Refresh creatives for retargeting; introduce new testimonials or photos
- **Week 4:** Review CPL, adjust audience targeting, prepare next month's content
