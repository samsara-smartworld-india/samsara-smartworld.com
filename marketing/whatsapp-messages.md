## Usage Notes

**Best Practices:**
- Send during business hours (9 AM - 7 PM IST) for highest open rates
- Personalise the greeting with the recipient's name when possible
- Attach 2-3 property photos with each message for visual impact
- Use WhatsApp Business for professional sender identity and quick replies
- Set up auto-reply for after-hours enquiries
- Add property photos to WhatsApp Business catalogue
